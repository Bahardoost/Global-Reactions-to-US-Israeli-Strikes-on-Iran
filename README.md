# Global-Reactions-to-US-Israeli-Strikes-on-Iran
Did wealth dictate politics in the 2026 Iran War? This study analyzes the positions of 62 nations during the conflict, using Excel to model data and test if GDP influences political alignment. Results reveal that strategic interests outweighed economic power in shaping global responses.

2026 Iran War Analysis: Geopolitical Stances

Overview
This research analyzes the political stances of 62 countries during the 2026 Iran War (Feb–June 2026). The project explores the correlation between global economic power (GDP) and political alignment (Support vs. Opposition vs. Neutrality).

Key Findings
* Political Breakdown:** Only 11.3% of countries supported the military strikes; 88.7% remained neutral or condemned the action.
* Economic Influence:** GDP shows almost no influence on political positioning (CORREL score of 0.11).
* Driving Factors:** Geography, history, and strategic interests were the primary determinants of political responses.
